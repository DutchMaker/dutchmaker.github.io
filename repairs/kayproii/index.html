Repair notes | Kaypro II

Disk drive head not moving

Possible cause:

  • If the stepper tries to move, but nothing happens, the mechanics are probably stuck because all lubricant dried up.
  • If stepper does not move at all and this happens for both disk drives, the FDC (Floppy Disk Controller) chip (FD 1719) may be broken.

Fix:

  • Make sure all mechanic parts are well lubricated.
  • Try swapping the drives by moving the jumpers from drive A to drive B. If the exact same thing happens with drive B, it's probably not a drive issue.
  • Replace the FDC chip with a known working chip.
