Repair notes | PET 2001

Loads of information found here: http://www.zimmers.net/anonftp/pub/cbm/firmware/computers/pet/.

Garbage screen

Possible cause: Broken ROM

Fix:

  • Burn a new ROM chip.
  • I only had 2732 chips so made an adapter to fit it in the 2532 sockets of the PET.

Possible cause: Reset circuit broken (RES on the 6502 stays low)

Fix:

  • Replace the 555 timer chip and its capacitors.

Screen jittery

Possible cause: Broken/damaged potentiometer

Fix:

  • Replace it.

Possible cause: Broken solder joints

Fix:

  • Inspect and fixt joints
